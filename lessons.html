Lessons

Whether you are a beginner or just looking to improve and expand your Musical skills, Guitar Arts Music Lessons at Norman's Rare Guitars will teach you how to play with an effective technique and gain the knowlege that you need to feel at home with your instrument. We offer professional private instruction for:


Instruments


Acoustic Guitar, Electric Guitar, Bass, Classical &
Flamenco Guitar, 5 Strink Banjo, Mandolin, and Dulcimer.


Styles


Rock, Pop, Blues, Folk, Classical and Flamenco, Jazz,
Country, and Bluegrass.


Our experienced professional instructers are patient, positive, and respect each students' musical tastes. We know how to design the best lesson program for your particular needs and interests. You will get immediate answers to your questions and your instructor can quickly correct any bad playing habits that can hurt your progress and joy of playing music. In our 1 on 1 teacher to student private lessons, our instructors can teach you directly in a way that Group lessons, books, videos, computer or online courses never could.


We accept students from ages 7 years and older.


Our rates start at $30.00 per 1/2 hr.


Lessons are scheduled on a weekly basis.
